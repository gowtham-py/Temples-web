Incredible Temples of India

Meenakshi Temple

Location: Madurai, Tamil Nadu

Opening Hours: 5 AM - 12 PM, 4 PM - 10 PM

Dress Code: Traditional wear

Rituals: Abhishekam, Alangaram

Built By: King Kulasekara Pandya

Built In: 6th century

Story: Dedicated to Meenakshi (Parvati) and Sundareswarar (Shiva)

Style: Dravidian Architecture

Rating: 4.8/5

Brihadeeswarar Temple

Location: Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu

Opening Hours: 6 AM - 12 PM, 4 PM - 8:30 PM

Dress Code: Traditional

Rituals: Pooja, Homam

Built By: Raja Raja Chola I

Built In: 1010 AD

Story: Lord Shiva in the form of Brihadeeswara

Style: Chola Architecture

Rating: 4.9/5

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Location: Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Opening Hours: 3 AM - 11 PM

Dress Code: Traditional preferred

Rituals: Mangala Aarti, Rudrabhishek

Built By: Ahilyabai Holkar

Built In: 1780

Story: One of the 12 Jyotirlingas

Style: Nagara Architecture

Rating: 4.7/5

Jagannath Temple

Location: Puri, Odisha

Opening Hours: 5 AM - 10 PM

Dress Code: Traditional attire

Rituals: Bhoga, Aarti, Rath Yatra

Built By: King Anantavarman Chodaganga

Built In: 12th Century

Story: Home of Lord Jagannath

Style: Kalinga Architecture

Rating: 4.8/5
